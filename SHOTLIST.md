### Capture tips
- Before recording, hit **Reset** so the guided run starts at the default $50k / $650.
- Shots 6 (sliders) and 8 (reallocation) are the two that prove the math is real — give them room.
- For a silent/GIF version, shots 4, 6, and 8 tell the whole story.
- Disable OS notifications; full-screen the browser; hide the bookmarks bar.
